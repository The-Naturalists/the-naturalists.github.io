The Naturalists

IISc Undergraduate biology club

Welcome to the website of The Naturalists, the IISc Undergraduate biology club. We are a small group of undergraduates, who are interested in biology and its different aspects and wish to explore the subject as much as we can. We aim to gain the most we can from the peer pool by arranging regular discussions among ourselves and presenting our views on different modern topics. Also, talks on different biological fields by professors will be arranged and we plan to go out occasionally on some trips.
