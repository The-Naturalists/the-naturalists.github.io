The Naturalists

IISc Undergraduate biology club

Welcome to the website of The Naturalists, the IISc Undergraduate biology club. We are a small group of undergraduates, who are interested in biology and its different aspects and wish to explore the subject as much as we can. Our activities include presentation by professors and seniors, group discussions about specific biological topics, and ocassional trips.
